Privacy Policy

Here's the deal: you probably don't want me to collect any information about you. Well, that's perfect, because I don't want it. The iOS applications WaveGuide and SOTA Goat only store enough information to keep their primary functionality working, and not much more.

The following information may be stored:

  • Your name and callsign (if you have logged into SOTAwatch3)
  • Push notification preferences (if configured)
  • A timestamp when the app was most recently launched

The following information will NEVER be stored:

  • Your e-mail address
  • Third-party account passwords

Any questions may be directed to schrockwell@gmail.com or @WW1X.
